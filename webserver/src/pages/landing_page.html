Preventing Disaster,
One Fire at a Time
Forest Fires cause billions of dollars of damage each year, not to mention the countless lives that are lost every year to these preventable disasters. Our dream is to one day end Forest Fires before they are allowed to start. We'll acheive this using a fleet of small drones that will survey a preprogrammed area for signs of fire, and report back any suspicious findings to firefighters, allowing them to put out the small blaze before it has a chance to spread. These drones will be cheap, and easy to operate through a website, forgoing the need for human operators until a fire is found.
